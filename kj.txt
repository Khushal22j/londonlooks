Case Study

Your company, TechNova Inc., a software development company, is currently working on a product launch for a new AI-powered tool designed to improve workplace productivity. The project was initially on track, but due to unforeseen delays such as technical issues with integration and the unexpected departure of a key team member, the project timeline is now at risk.

As the project manager, you need additional resources (such as budget, personnel, or time) to ensure that the product launch can happen successfully.

Your task is to draft an email to your manager requesting additional resources (team members and additional budget) to address these challenges and keep the project on track for its upcoming release.


---

Current Situation

The product’s features are behind schedule due to technical issues.

A key team member left, and no immediate replacement has been hired yet.

The current project deadline is this Friday.

The team is working overtime, but without more resources the deadline may be missed.



---

Request

Request additional budget for hiring contractors or full-time employees to speed up development.

Request more time to meet the original product quality standards and suggest a new realistic deadline.



---

Objectives

1. Clearly explain the reason for the delay and why the project needs extra resources.


2. Propose a solution, including a specific request for additional budget and personnel.


3. Show the positive impact that these resources will have on the project’s success and company revenue.




---

Focus Areas

Tone: Professional and respectful.

Clarity: Clearly explain the problem, resource needs, and expected impact.

Conciseness: Keep the email brief while providing enough detail to justify the request.



---

Instructions

Write an email to your manager requesting additional resources for the AI-powered tool project.

Structure the email well, maintain a professional tone, and make a clear request.

Focus on clarity and conciseness so the manager can easily understand the situation.



---

Key Details to Include

Context: The product delay and reasons (technical issues and team member leaving).

Specific request: Additional budget, personnel, or more time.

Impact: How these resources will help the project meet the new deadline and avoid further risks.
